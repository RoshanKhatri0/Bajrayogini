Our Projects

See Dance Championships

Bajrayogini has organized the See Dance Championships for 4 seasons since 2018, focusing on students who have completed the Secondary Education Examination (SEE) and have dance skills. The championships provide a platform for talented individuals to showcase their abilities and pursue their passion for dance. Bajrayogini offers scholarships to deserving students to support them financially in pursuing their dance dreams. The program has benefited numerous students over the seasons, providing them with opportunities for growth and artistic development.
The championships foster a sense of community and collaboration among participants, offering workshops and performances to enhance skills and create connections. Bajrayogini's dedication to organizing the event demonstrates their commitment to promoting arts and culture in Nepal. The See Dance Championships have become a significant annual event, attracting attention from dance enthusiasts and professionals.

Folk Dance Camp

Bajrayogini offers immersive dance experiences, guided by expert instructors, where participants engage in interactive workshops and collaborative performances. They explore traditional dances from diverse cultures worldwide, mastering intricate footwork and embracing the beauty of each dance form. These experiences provide a unique opportunity to learn, refine techniques, and connect with fellow dancers. Bajrayogini's aim is to create a space where individuals can deepen their cultural knowledge and enhance their dance proficiency, fostering a greater appreciation for global dance traditions. Participants of Bajrayogini's immersive dance experiences gain a deeper understanding of the cultural significance behind each dance form. The expert instructors provide guidance and expertise, ensuring a comprehensive learning journey. Through these workshops and performances, dancers not only refine their skills but also foster a sense of unity and appreciation for the rich tapestry of global dance heritage.

Dance Performance in National Programs

In addition to the See Dance Championships and immersive dance experiences, Bajrayogini Academy has made significant contributions to the national dance scene in Nepal. The academy has been honored to showcase its talent by providing opening and closing dance performances at the 9th National Games organized by the Sports Association of Nepal. These prestigious national events serve as a platform for athletes and performers from across the country to display their skills and represent their regions. Bajrayogini Academy's involvement in the opening and closing ceremonies highlights the recognition and respect it has gained for its exceptional dance performances. Moreover, the academy has been actively involved in various other national programs, where it has conducted captivating dance performances. These events are significant milestones in the cultural calendar of Nepal, drawing attention from a diverse audience, including government officials, dignitaries, and the general public. By participating in these national programs, Bajrayogini Academy not only promotes the art of dance but also contributes to the cultural fabric of the nation. Their performances showcase the beauty and diversity of traditional and contemporary dance forms, leaving a lasting impression on spectators. Overall, Bajrayogini Academy's involvement in national events underscores its reputation as a leading institution in the dance community, and its commitment to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Nepal through captivating performances.

+2 dance championships

Bajrayogini Academy is gearing up to launch a new dance competition aimed specifically at +2 passout students in Nepal. This exciting program provides a platform for talented individuals with a passion for dance to showcase their skills and pave the way for a future built upon their dance abilities. The competition is designed to identify and nurture exceptional dance talent among +2 passout students. It invites participants who possess the necessary dance skills and aspire to pursue a career in the field. The program offers them an opportunity to exhibit their talent and gain recognition within the dance community. By organizing this competition, Bajrayogini Academy aims to empower young dancers and encourage them to pursue their dreams. It provides a supportive and competitive environment where participants can further refine their skills, receive guidance from industry experts, and gain valuable performance experience. The upcoming dance competition has generated considerable excitement among the dance enthusiasts in Nepal. It is anticipated to attract a diverse range of participants from different schools and regions across the country, showcasing the rich talent pool that exists among +2 passout students. Through this program, Bajrayogini Academy aspires to contribute to the growth and development of the dance industry in Nepal, fostering a generation of skilled and dedicated dancers who can shape the future of dance in the country.

About Us
