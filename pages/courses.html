Our Courses

Dance Courses

Hiphop

We incorporation of various styles like locking, popping, house, krump, and breaking. Mention the dynamic and expressive nature of hip-hop dance and its cultural significance. Emphasize the elements of self-expression, creativity, and freestyle battles within the hip-hop dance community.

Zumba

We offer Zumba which is accessible to people of all fitness levels and dance abilities. Highlight the benefits of burning calories, improving cardiovascular fitness, toning muscles, and increasing coordination. Stress the importance of finding certified instructors for proper guidance and instruction.

Contemporary

Join our institute for versatile and expressive contemporary dance classes. Explore fluid movements, emotive storytelling, and artistic interpretation while developing strength, flexibility, and grace. Our experienced instructors provide expert guidance in a supportive environment. Experience the joy of movement and embark on a transformative dance journey with us.

Bharatnatyam

Explore the grace of Bharatanatyam, a classical Indian dance form with intricate footwork, expressive gestures, and captivating storytelling. Join our institute for classes suitable for all ages and levels. Learn traditional techniques, cultural understanding, and enhance posture, coordination, and musicality. Immerse yourself in the rich heritage of Indian dance and experience the profound artistry of Bharatanatyam with us.

Kathak

Discover the enchanting world of Kathak, a classical Indian dance form combining storytelling, intricate footwork, graceful movements, and rhythmic patterns. Join our institute for Kathak classes, led by experienced instructors fostering technique and artistic expression. Explore the rich history, cultural nuances, and mesmerizing spins while developing poise, musicality, and stage presence. Embark on a captivating journey of self-discovery and immerse yourself in the magic of Kathak with us.

Nepali Folk dance

The Nepali Folk Dance course at Bajrayogini Academy offers structured training in traditional dance forms, covering history, techniques, and performances. Students gain knowledge of Nepali folk dances and develop their skills under expert guidance.

About Us
“Dance is not only for entertainment; it is also a part of education.”

Bajrayogini Dance & Music Academy Pvt. Ltd. was founded by Mahesh Godar after the name of Goddess Bajrayogini, this name comes from Charya dances. It focuses on innovative concepts with a fresh approach. We are specialists in high-class Dance championships, corporate events, TV documentaries/magazines, music films, and corporate films.
